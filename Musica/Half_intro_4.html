Overview


With more than 10+ years of experience, we are working towards spreading the beauty of hindustani classical singing in youth. Based on Gwalior gharana paddhati, we impart the traditional nuances of signing. Journey of a student begins with voice culturing, pitch corrections, range extensions and then going deep into the ocean of Ragas.
