About us

Music is an art form .and the cultural activity, whose medium we call as sound . Billions of people love music and many of them opt it as their profession and have achieved so many heights in their life. There are different forms of music., Especially in India, we have so many forms of music. As India is a diverse country we have many languages and each language has its own forms of music. Music has its own syllabus and it must be learnt by the proper tutor to excel in the music field. Vocals and Instruments are to types of it. Some people opt vocal and some opt Instruments based on their interests. In India we have some individuals and some music foundation teaching music for the aspirants. My project Musica is a webpage that can help all those people who wants to join the best music class near them and make best use of the suggestions provided by the tutors. Currrently we have no website that particularly made to provide the details to the people about the music courses provided by the Academy or any classes. I took this problem statement because I have personally faced this problem when I arrived Bangalore for the first time. I was eager to join the best music class in Bangalore but when I googled, I didn’t find one best website that would provide me the details or contact the tutor. I was not able to join the music class because of lack of information. Musica is the one stop solution to all the problems that I faced and to all those who have faced the same problem. This website helps all those who want to learn music by the best tutor near them as they would find all the necessary details, location, fees, courses, photos, contact details of the tutor. This will also help the Music teachers to get more students. Music academy can register their class on our website by contacting us or by filling the form provided in the web page. I have developed this project by using skills like HTML,CSS,PHP,JS and Bootstrap4. Most of my code includes the snippet of the Bootstrap4 to make my website responsive and make it available even in small screens like mobile phones to big devices like laptop, Homedesktop. This webpage looks professional and there are 4 categories and all four categories has different Sub category pages. More Information about the Website is provided in this report. Music Teachers will get a platform to adverstise their classes and it will help them to reach more and more learners.. In India Music has lot of followers and many of them want a perfect platform to search the right music class and academy to showcase their talent.
